This guide helps you start your print-on-demand journey with Rabfy. Follow these easy steps. Each one includes a video to guide you visually.

After you complete this guide, you will have a live store ready to market and scale.

<Note>
  **Prerequisites**: Before you begin, [**create an account**](https://dashboard.rabfy.com/sign-up) and complete onboarding.
</Note>

## **Step 1: Create Your Rabfy Account & Store**

Start by opening your seller account.

- Visit[ <u>https://dashboard.rabfy.com/sign-up</u>](https://dashboard.rabfy.com/sign-up)
- Fill in your name, email, password
- Click continue and verify your email
- Then give your store name and country (from where your store will be operated)
- Click create store
- Log in to your Rabfy dashboard
