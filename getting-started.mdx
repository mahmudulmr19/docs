# Getting Started

Welcome to Rabfy! Our platform helps you build a profitable Print-on-Demand business with zero inventory hassles. This guide will help you launch your custom product store in just a few simple steps.

## What is Rabfy?

Rabfy is a Print-on-Demand (POD) platform where creators can upload custom designs and sell products without managing inventory or shipping. When a customer places an order, we handle everything — from printing to doorstep delivery.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A modern web browser
- An internet connection
- Creative designs or artwork ready to upload
- A Rabfy account (you can sign up for free at [rabfy.com](https://rabfy.com))

## How to Begin Building Your Store

### 1. Sign Up & Create Your Account

Getting started is completely free with no upfront costs or inventory requirements. Simply create your account to begin your journey.

### 2. Create Your Storefront

Build a clean & professional storefront on your domain using our no-code store builder:

- Choose fonts, colors, and layouts that make your brand instantly recognizable
- Drag, drop, and launch — zero coding needed
- Customize your store to reflect your unique brand

### 3. Design Your Products

Upload your artwork and create products using our user-friendly product builder:

- Upload your custom designs
- Apply designs to 30+ premium products (t-shirts, hoodies, phone cases, mugs, tote bags, and more)
- Preview live mockups before publishing
- Set your selling prices using our profit calculator

### 4. Launch & Start Selling

Once your products are ready:

- Publish your store and products
- Share your store with customers
- We handle printing, packaging, and shipping (4-8 day delivery)
- You focus on creating and marketing while we fulfill orders

## Available Products

You can brand and sell 30+ premium products across categories:
